Who we are

Ethiopian Minerals Corporation is a state-owned enterprise established in 1942 tasked with the exploration, development, processing and marketing of mineral resources in Ethiopia.

Having been established in different iterations our corporation was established as the pioneer mechanised mining operator in Africa. We're engaged in the exploration, mining, acquisition and processing of various mineral commodities like gemstones, gold, petroleum, natural gas and various industrial metals and minerals.

Why we care

We've over 80 years of heritage that gave birth to mining in Ethiopia. We've established mining towns and cities in various part of the country to benefit the locals and continue to do so. As a state owned enterprise we strive to give back to the host communities and local economy benefiting from what the land has gifted us.

Over 80 years of history

Since 1942 we’ve been contributing to the Ethiopian mineral Industry, community, and economic development. Our corporation’s experience and expertise in the space is demonstrated by our rich history spanning over 80 years.
