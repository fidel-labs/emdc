Who we are

Ethiopian Minerals Development Corporation established in 1935 is a state owned enterprise tasked with the exploration, development, processing and marketing of mineral resources in Ethiopia.

Having been established in different iterations our corporation was established in 1935 being the pioneer mechanised mining operator in Africa. We're engaged in the exploration, mining, acquisition and processing of various mineral commodities like gemstones, gold, petroleum, natural gas and various industrial metals and minerals.
